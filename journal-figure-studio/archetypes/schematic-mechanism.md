# Schematic / Mechanism

For workflows, experimental timelines, mechanism diagrams, graphical abstracts, and conceptual summaries.

## Required Data Contract

- Entities, states, process order, causal claims, and what is evidence versus hypothesis.
- Source figure/data links for any evidence-derived element.

## Visual Encoding

- Use arrows with explicit semantics: activation, inhibition, flow, temporal order, association.
- Keep hierarchy clear and labels short.
- Separate schematic visuals from data evidence panels.

## Integrity

- Do not generate or alter scientific evidence images.
- Clearly label speculative models or summaries.

## QA Checks

- Decorative complexity can obscure mechanism.
- Arrows can overstate causality.
- Graphical summaries must not imply evidence absent from data panels.
