# Mixed Multipanel

For asymmetric figures combining charts, images, tables, schematics, and quantification.

## Required Data Contract

- Panel list with data source, message, archetype, and evidence role for each panel.
- Shared variables, sample definitions, and figure-level claim.

## Visual Encoding

- Use a hero/support/context hierarchy.
- Align panels to a stable grid, but allow asymmetric space for the main evidence.
- Keep legends and annotations local when possible.

## QA Checks

- Reading order can become unclear.
- Panels can become too small to read at final size.
- Redundant panels should be merged, removed, or moved to supplement.
