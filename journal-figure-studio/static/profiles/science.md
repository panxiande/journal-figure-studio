# Science / AAAS Style Profile

Use for Science-style layout and story ordering. Do not present unverified dimensions or formats as official requirements.

## Rule Layers

### required

- rule: Hard figure submission requirements must be verified in the latest AAAS/Science author guide for the target article type.
  source_type: official
  confidence: medium
  last_verified: 2026-06-16
  source_url: https://www.science.org/content/page/instructions-preparing-initial-manuscript
  applies_to: all

### recommended

- Build compact, high-information figures with clear story progression.
- Prefer direct, concise annotations and avoid decorative effects.
- Keep captions and detailed methods outside the figure.

### style_observed

- Tight multipanel layouts, strong main-result ordering, economical labels, and high data density.

### unknown_or_verify

- Exact dimensions, file formats, fonts, dpi, and upload constraints are access- or article-type-dependent and must be checked before submission.

## Defaults

- Canvas: use general-publication double-column defaults until verified.
- Typography: Arial/Helvetica, 6-8 pt body, bold panel labels.
- Export: SVG/PDF editable sources plus PNG/TIFF preview; verify final submission format.
