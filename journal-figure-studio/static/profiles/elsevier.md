# Elsevier Baseline Profile

Use for broad Elsevier artwork requirements when a specific journal guide is not yet known.

## Rule Layers

### required

- rule: Artwork requirements vary by journal; verify the exact Guide for Authors.
  source_type: publisher
  confidence: high
  last_verified: 2026-06-16
  source_url: https://www.elsevier.com/researcher/author/artwork-and-media-instructions
  applies_to: all

### recommended

- Use EPS/PDF for vector art and TIFF/PNG/JPEG for raster art as allowed by the journal.
- Use 300 dpi for halftone, 500 dpi for combination art, and 1000 dpi for line art when applicable.
- Submit logical figure files named in article order.

### style_observed

- Clear axis labeling, moderate whitespace, concise annotations.

### unknown_or_verify

- Exact sizing, fonts, format list, and graphical-abstract policies depend on journal.
