# Nature Profile

Use for Nature and Nature-family style unless the user specifies a particular Nature Portfolio journal guide.

## Rule Layers

### required

- rule: Single-column figures are 89 mm wide; double-column figures are 183 mm wide; maximum height is 170 mm.
  source_type: official
  confidence: high
  last_verified: 2026-06-16
  source_url: https://research-figure-guide.nature.com/figures/preparing-figures-our-specifications/
  applies_to: main figure
- rule: Keep text editable and use standard fonts rather than outlining text.
  source_type: official
  confidence: high
  last_verified: 2026-06-16
  source_url: https://research-figure-guide.nature.com/figures/building-and-exporting-figure-panels/
  applies_to: all

### recommended

- Use 5-7 pt text at final size.
- Use 8 pt bold, non-italic, lowercase panel labels.
- Avoid decorative elements, drop shadows, patterned fills, crowded labels, and red/green-only encoding.
- Use RGB for images.

### style_observed

- Dense but quiet layouts, strong panel hierarchy, small direct labels, no chart titles.

### unknown_or_verify

- Article-type-specific or journal-specific variants must be checked against the latest target guide.

## Defaults

- Canvas: 89 mm single or 183 mm double; max height 170 mm.
- Typography: Arial/Helvetica, 6 pt body, 8 pt bold lowercase panel labels.
- Palette: Okabe-Ito categorical, perceptual sequential maps, restrained grayscale image panels.
- Export: PDF/SVG editable text plus high-resolution TIFF/PNG for raster previews.
- Integrity: strong microscopy/blot/source-data review.
