# Medical Journal Profile

MVP profile. Use for clinical evidence figures such as forest plots, Kaplan-Meier curves, CONSORT flows, and subgroup analyses.

## Rule Layers

### recommended

- Emphasize sample size, confidence intervals, risk tables, cohort definitions, and reporting framework alignment.
- Prefer clinically interpretable effect measures over decorative summaries.
- Label axes and units plainly.

### style_observed

- Conservative typography, high contrast, minimal color, direct labels, strong table-plot hybrids.

### unknown_or_verify

- Exact NEJM/Lancet/JAMA/BMJ figure requirements and reporting checklist details must be verified for the target journal and study design.
