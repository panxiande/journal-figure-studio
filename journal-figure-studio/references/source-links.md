# Source Links

Record source URLs and verification dates in profiles. Final submission should always be checked against the latest official author guide.

- Nature research figure guide: https://research-figure-guide.nature.com/
- Nature figure specifications: https://research-figure-guide.nature.com/figures/preparing-figures-our-specifications/
- Nature building/exporting panels: https://research-figure-guide.nature.com/figures/building-and-exporting-figure-panels/
- PLOS ONE figure guidelines: https://journals.plos.org/plosone/s/figures
- Elsevier artwork and author guides: https://www.elsevier.com/researcher/author/artwork-and-media-instructions
- Cell Press guide pages vary by journal and should be verified for the target title.
- JCB figure/video guidelines: https://rupress.org/jcb/pages/fig-vid-guidelines
- JCB submission guidelines: https://rupress.org/jcb/pages/submission-guidelines

Use `unknown_or_verify` when access is blocked, the journal title is unspecified, or guidance varies by article type.
