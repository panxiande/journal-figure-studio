# Statistics Legend

Require enough information for a reader to reconstruct what a statistical mark means.

## Minimum Fields

- n and what n represents.
- Biological versus technical repeats.
- Center value: mean, median, geometric mean, model estimate.
- Spread/uncertainty: SD, SEM, CI, IQR, range.
- Test/model and sidedness.
- Multiple-comparison correction.
- Exact p value or threshold convention.
- Effect size when meaningful.

## Anti-Patterns

- Bar plus error only when raw points or distribution are feasible.
- Technical replicates plotted as independent biological replicates.
- p-value stars without tests or comparison brackets.
- Error bars without definition.
- Overstated claims from underpowered or exploratory data.
