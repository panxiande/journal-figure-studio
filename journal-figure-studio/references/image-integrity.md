# Image Integrity

Use this for microscopy, histology, gel/blot, photographic, and source-image workflows.

## Required Notes

- Original source-image path and acquisition metadata when available.
- Crop coordinates or description.
- Processing: brightness, contrast, gamma, denoising, background subtraction, pseudocolor.
- Composite channels and LUT choices.
- Scale bar length and pixel calibration.
- Splices, reused controls, lanes removed, zoom crops, or non-adjacent regions.

## Prohibitions

- Do not use generative AI to create, repair, extend, erase, or alter scientific evidence images.
- Do not remove background, bands, cells, artifacts, or fields of view in ways that change interpretation.
- Do not hide splices or reused regions.
