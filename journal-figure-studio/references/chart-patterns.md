# Chart Patterns

## Multipanel Ordering

Use left-to-right, top-to-bottom reading unless a journal or figure type has a strong reason to differ. Put setup/context before the main result when needed; otherwise let the strongest evidence lead.

## Dense Scientific Figures

- Prefer direct labels over large legends when space is tight.
- Use shared axes for repeated small multiples.
- Remove background grids unless they aid quantitative reading.
- Keep statistical annotations close to the comparison they describe.

## Common Improvements

- Replace bar-only summaries with dot plus summary, box/violin, or estimation plots.
- Split overpacked heatmaps into main map plus annotation tracks.
- Pair image plates with quantification panels when a claim depends on measured differences.
